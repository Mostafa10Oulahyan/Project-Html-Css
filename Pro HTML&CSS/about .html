About us

Libra Tech is an educational library that provides a wide
range of resources including books, magazines, and digital
materials, to support
self-learning and skill development in a conducive research

and learning environment.

Our objectives are to:

Empower self-learning through comprehensive educational resources.
Enhance research skills and critical thinking.
Provide an interactive learning environment that encourages exploration
and innovation.
Develop practical skills in various fields to support professional success.
Ensure comprehensive access to modern digital content.
Encourage a culture of reading and organize ongoing educational events.
